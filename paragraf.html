First Time Go to Lombok

One year ago, my family and I prepared to go on holiday to Lombok.
We left home at nine oclock a.m., and left home by car It takes 4 hours to get to Ketapang port. On the night trip, it was very quiet, and there were not many vehicles. After arriving at Ketapang port, take a ship to Bali. A short trip from Bali to Padang Bai port, then cross directly by ship. Around 8 am, we arrived at Lombok. I felt very happy even though I was exhausted on the trip. Our destination was Pamenang, stopping for a while to rest and eat together. Next, we headed to Gili Trawangan, which had to be crossed using a Javanese word called “Getek”, the Indonesian term for a small boat. My family and I checked into the hotel and rested.
